<p><p>The Tire and Rubber Lubricants market can be categorized into two main types: natural and synthetic lubricants. Natural lubricants are derived from organic sources, offering eco-friendly properties and excellent performance in various conditions. They help reduce friction and wear in rubber formulations. On the other hand, synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered and provide enhanced stability, superior thermal performance, and improved viscosity control. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing the manufacturing processes and performance of tires and rubber products.</p></p>
