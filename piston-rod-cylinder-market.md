<p><strong>The Piston Rod Cylinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting Cylinders</li><li>Double-Acting Cylinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piston rod cylinder market is primarily categorized into single-acting and double-acting cylinders. Single-acting cylinders utilize hydraulic or pneumatic pressure to extend the piston in one direction, relying on a spring or external force to retract. In contrast, double-acting cylinders apply pressure to both ends of the piston, enabling movement in both directions. This distinction affects their applications, with single-acting cylinders often used in simpler tasks, while double-acting cylinders are favored for more complex, force-intensive operations.</p></p>
