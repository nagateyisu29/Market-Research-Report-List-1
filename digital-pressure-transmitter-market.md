<p><p>The digital pressure transmitter market comprises several types, primarily including absolute pressure transmitters, differential pressure transmitters, and other specialized transmitters. Absolute pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, making them ideal for applications requiring precise pressure readings. Differential pressure transmitters measure the difference between two pressure points, commonly used in flow measurement and filter monitoring. Other market segments encompass various technologies and applications, such as gauge pressure transmitters and specialized industrial transmitters, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
