<p><p>The digital assistant market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based digital assistants operate via internet connectivity, allowing users to access features and data from anywhere, offering scalability and ease of updates. In contrast, on-premise digital assistants are installed directly on local servers, providing greater control and security for organizations but often requiring more maintenance. Each type suits different business needs, balancing convenience and resources against control and security considerations.</p></p>
