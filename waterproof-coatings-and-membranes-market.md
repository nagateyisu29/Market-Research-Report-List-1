<p><p>The waterproof coatings and membranes market includes various types such as PVC, EPDM, TPO, HDPE, and LDPE. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used for its durability and flexibility, suitable for roofs and foundations. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is favored for its excellent weather resistance, often used in roofing systems. TPO (Thermoplastic Olefin) is lightweight and energy-efficient, ideal for commercial roofing. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers rigorous protection against corrosion, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and suitable for various applications, including liners and membranes.</p></p>
