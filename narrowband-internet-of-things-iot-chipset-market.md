<p><ul><li>Stand-Alone</li><li>Guard Band</li><li>In-Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Narrowband Internet of Things (NB-IoT) chipset market comprises three types: Stand-Alone, Guard Band, and In-Band. </p><p>Stand-Alone operates on dedicated frequency bands, ensuring optimized performance for IoT devices without interference. Guard Band utilizes spare frequencies between two systems, providing a buffer that minimizes disruption while enhancing connectivity. In-Band integrates within existing LTE networks, leveraging existing infrastructure, which facilitates a smoother and cost-effective deployment for IoT applications. Each type caters to specific use cases, enhancing the scalability and efficiency of IoT networks.</p></p>
