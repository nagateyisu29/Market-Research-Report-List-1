<p><p>The surgical stapler market is categorized into two main types: laparoscopic staplers and open staplers. Laparoscopic staplers are designed for minimally invasive surgeries, allowing surgeons to join tissues through small incisions using specialized techniques. These staplers enhance precision and reduce recovery time. Open staplers, on the other hand, are used in traditional open surgeries where larger incisions are made. They provide strong and reliable tissue closure for various surgical procedures, catering to different patient needs and surgical contexts.</p></p>
