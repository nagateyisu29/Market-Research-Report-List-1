<p><p>A Once-through Steam Generator (OTSG) is a type of heat exchanger used primarily in power generation and industrial processes. Unlike traditional steam boilers, which recycle water and steam in a closed loop, OTSGs convert water to steam in a single pass, enhancing efficiency and reducing footprint. This technology is particularly advantageous in applications requiring high-temperature and high-pressure steam, such as in gas and steam combined cycle power plants.</p><p>The Once-through Steam Generator (OTSG) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
