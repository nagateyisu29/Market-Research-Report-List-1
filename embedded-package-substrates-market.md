<p><strong>The Embedded Package Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Device Embedding</li><li>Active Device Embedding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Package Substrates market is divided into two main types: Passive Device Embedding and Active Device Embedding. Passive Device Embedding involves integrating passive components, such as resistors and capacitors, directly into the substrate, enhancing space efficiency and electrical performance. In contrast, Active Device Embedding integrates active components like integrated circuits, allowing reduced package size and improved functionality. Both types aim to optimize device performance and miniaturization, playing a crucial role in the advancement of electronics and packaging technologies.</p></p>
