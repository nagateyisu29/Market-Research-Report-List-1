<p><p>Ablation technologies encompass various techniques for tissue destruction, each suited for different medical applications. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat generated by radio waves to target abnormal tissues. Laser/light ablation employs focused light energy to vaporize tissues. Cryoablation involves freezing tissue to induce cell death. Microwave ablation utilizes electromagnetic waves to generate heat for tissue ablation. Hydrothermal ablation employs heated water to destroy tissues. Other methods may include chemical or ultrasound-based techniques, addressing diverse therapeutic needs across specialties.</p></p>
