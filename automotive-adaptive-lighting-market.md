<p><p>The automotive adaptive lighting market consists of three primary types: Halogen, LED, and Xenon. Halogen lights are traditional, offering warmth and affordability, but are less energy-efficient. LED lights provide longer lifespan, higher brightness, and energy efficiency, making them popular in modern vehicles. Xenon lights, or HID, offer superior illumination and longer range but are typically more expensive and require complex installations. Each type contributes uniquely to vehicle safety and visibility, catering to varying consumer preferences and technological advancements.</p></p>
