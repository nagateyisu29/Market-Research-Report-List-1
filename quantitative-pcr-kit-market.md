<p><strong>The Quantitative PCR Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Probe-Based</li><li>SYBR Green</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quantitative PCR (qPCR) Kit market is primarily divided into two types: probe-based and SYBR Green methods. Probe-based kits utilize specific fluorescent probes to detect target DNA, offering high specificity and sensitivity, essential for precise quantification. SYBR Green kits, on the other hand, use a dye that binds to any double-stranded DNA, providing a more straightforward and cost-effective option. Both types serve vital roles in research, clinical diagnostics, and genetic analysis, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
