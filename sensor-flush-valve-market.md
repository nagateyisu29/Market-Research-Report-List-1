<p><p>The sensor flush valve market encompasses two primary types: concealed and exposed flush valves. Concealed flush valves are installed within the wall, offering a sleek, minimalist appearance while maintaining hygiene and reducing vandalism. In contrast, exposed flush valves are visible and mounted outside the wall, providing easy access for maintenance and repair. Both types utilize automatic sensors to enhance water efficiency and improve user experience, catering to the growing demand for hands-free solutions in public restrooms and commercial facilities.</p></p>
