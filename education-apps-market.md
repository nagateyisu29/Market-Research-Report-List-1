<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The education apps market can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based education apps are hosted on remote servers, allowing access from any device with internet connectivity. This model offers scalability and automatic updates, making it user-friendly for educators and students. In contrast, on-premise education apps are installed locally on an institution's server, providing greater control over data and security. However, they require more maintenance and may involve higher upfront costs. Each type serves different institutional needs.</p></p>
