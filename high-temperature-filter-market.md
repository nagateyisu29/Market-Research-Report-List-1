<p><p>The high-temperature filter market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) filters are known for chemical resistance and thermal stability, while P84 (Polyimide) filters excel in high-temperature and oil-resistant environments. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) filters offer exceptional non-stick properties and chemical resistance. Nomex, made from aramid fibers, is ideal for high-temperature applications in industrial settings. PSA (Polyester) filters are versatile for various industries, and fiberglass filters provide durability and high-efficiency filtration in extreme conditions.</p></p>
