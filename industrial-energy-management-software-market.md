<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Energy Management Software (IEMS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and premise-based solutions. Cloud-based IEMS leverages remote servers for data storage and processing, offering scalability, accessibility, and real-time insights from anywhere. In contrast, premise-based solutions are installed locally on company servers, providing organizations with greater control over their data and compliance with security protocols, but often lacking the flexibility of cloud options. Both approaches aim to optimize energy usage, reduce costs, and improve sustainability.</p></p>
