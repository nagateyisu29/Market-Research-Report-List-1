<p><p>Laser levels for the construction market include several types, each serving specific purposes. Dot laser levels project a single point for precise alignment. Line laser levels emit horizontal or vertical lines, ideal for framing and layout work. Rotary laser levels create 360-degree visibility, suited for large, outdoor projects like grading. Torpedo laser levels are compact and easily portable, designed for quick horizontal or vertical adjustments in tight spaces. Each type enhances accuracy and efficiency in construction tasks.</p></p>
