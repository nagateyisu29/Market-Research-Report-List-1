<p><p>Trenchless pipe rehabilitation is a method used for repairing and upgrading underground pipelines without the need for extensive excavation. The growing need for infrastructure maintenance, particularly in urban areas where traditional excavation can cause significant disruptions, has fueled market interest in trenchless technologies.</p><p>The Trenchless Pipe Rehabilitation Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as robotic inspection tools and better materials, are leading to enhanced efficiency and effectiveness in rehabilitation processes. The trend toward adopting smart water management systems is also influencing the market, as utilities seek more reliable methods for maintaining their water and wastewater networks.
