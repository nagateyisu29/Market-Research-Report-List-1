<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) market is divided into two primary types: LiDAR-based and LiDAR+Vision-based. LiDAR-based AMRs utilize laser scanning technology to create precise 2D and 3D maps of their environment, enabling them to navigate efficiently in varying conditions. In contrast, LiDAR+Vision-based AMRs combine LiDAR with visual sensors, such as cameras, to enhance perception, object recognition, and obstacle avoidance. This dual approach improves navigation accuracy and operational safety, making them suitable for complex environments.</p></p>
