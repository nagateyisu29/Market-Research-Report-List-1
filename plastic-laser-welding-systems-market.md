<p><ul><li>YAG Laser Welding Machine</li><li>CO2 Laser Welding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic laser welding systems market primarily comprises two types: YAG laser welding machines and CO2 laser welding machines. YAG laser welding machines utilize neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet lasers, known for their precision and ability to weld small, intricate plastic parts with minimal thermal distortion. CO2 laser welding machines employ carbon dioxide lasers, effective for larger components and offering high-speed processing with versatile material compatibility. Both technologies are crucial in industries like automotive, electronics, and medical for achieving strong, seamless joints.</p></p>
