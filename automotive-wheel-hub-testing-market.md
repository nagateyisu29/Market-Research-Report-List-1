<p><p>The automotive wheel hub testing market encompasses various testing types to ensure reliability and safety. Performance testing evaluates the hub's ability to withstand dynamic loads, while PCD (Pitch Circle Diameter) testing measures the hub's bolt pattern compatibility for proper fitting. Inertial resistance testing assesses how well the hub can resist spinning forces during operation. Other testing methods may include fatigue, thermal, and vibration analysis, all aimed at ensuring wheel hubs meet rigorous performance and safety standards in automotive applications.</p></p>
