<p><strong>The Dental Air Filtration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ULPA Air Filtration</li><li>HEPA Air Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental air filtration market comprises two primary types: ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters and HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters. ULPA filters capture 99.999% of particles as small as 0.1 microns, making them ideal for highly sensitive environments like dental clinics. HEPA filters, while slightly less efficient, still capture 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, providing effective protection against airborne contaminants. Both types are crucial for maintaining hygienic conditions and ensuring patient safety in dental practices.</p></p>
