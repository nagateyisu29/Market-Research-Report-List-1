<p><ul><li>Low Expansion Foam Extinguishing System</li><li>Medium Expansion Foam Extinguishing System</li><li>High Expansion Foam Fire Extinguishing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foam fire suppression systems are categorized based on the expansion ratio of the foam they produce. Low expansion foam extinguishing systems generate foam with a low expansion ratio, ideal for localized fires, particularly in flammable liquid hazards. Medium expansion foam is versatile, suitable for both surface and sub-surface fires, often used in storage tank applications. High expansion foam systems produce a large volume of foam quickly, effectively filling spaces to suppress fires in confined areas, such as warehouses and underground parking.</p></p>
