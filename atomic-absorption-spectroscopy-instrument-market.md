<p><p>The Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (AAS) instrument market is categorized into three main types: Flame AAS, Graphite Furnace AAS, and Other technologies. Flame AAS utilizes a flame to vaporize the sample, allowing for the analysis of various elements quickly and at low costs. Graphite Furnace AAS offers higher sensitivity by using an electrically heated graphite tube to atomize samples, enabling the detection of trace elements. Other methods may include techniques like hydride generation or cold vapor, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
