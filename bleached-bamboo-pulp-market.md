<p><strong>The Bleached Bamboo Pulp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Caustic Soda Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bleached bamboo pulp market is primarily categorized into two processing types: the sulfate process and the caustic soda process. The sulfate process, also known as the Kraft process, utilizes a mixture of sodium sulfate and sodium hydroxide to produce high-strength pulp, making it suitable for various paper products. In contrast, the caustic soda process employs sodium hydroxide to digest bamboo fibers, resulting in a relatively lower environmental impact and a smoother pulp texture, ideal for fine papers and specialty applications.</p></p>
