<p><p>The DCP Pharma Grade market encompasses two primary types: Dibasic Anhydrous (DCPA) and Dicalcium Phosphate Dihydrate (DCPD). DCPA is a dehydrated form, commonly utilized as a calcium supplement and in pharmaceutical applications due to its low moisture content and stability. In contrast, DCPD is a hydrated form, often used in formulations for dietary supplements and pharmaceuticals, providing a stable source of calcium and phosphorus. Both types serve essential roles in health and nutrition sectors, enhancing product efficacy.</p></p>
