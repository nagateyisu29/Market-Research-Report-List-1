<p><p>The electric knife sharpener market is primarily categorized into two types based on their sharpening stages. Two-stage electric knife sharpeners typically feature a coarse grinding wheel for initial sharpening and a fine wheel for honing the blade's edge. In contrast, three-stage electric knife sharpeners add an additional stage, often incorporating a ceramic wheel for a finer finish, ensuring a sharper and more refined edge. Both types offer convenience and efficiency, appealing to both home cooks and professional chefs.</p></p>
