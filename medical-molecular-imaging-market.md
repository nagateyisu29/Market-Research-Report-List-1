<p><p>The medical molecular imaging market comprises various modalities that enhance diagnostic capabilities. MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) uses magnetic fields to provide detailed images of soft tissues. CT (Computed Tomography) combines X-ray images for cross-sectional views, helpful in identifying tumors. PET (Positron Emission Tomography) detects metabolic activity by using radioactive tracers, ideal for cancer monitoring. SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography) offers functional imaging, particularly in cardiology. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves for real-time imaging, widely used for examining various organs and monitoring pregnancies.</p></p>
