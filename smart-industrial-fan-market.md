<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Axial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Industrial Fan Market includes two primary types: centrifugal fans and axial fans. Centrifugal fans use a rotating impeller to increase air velocity, ideal for high-pressure applications like ventilation systems. In contrast, axial fans move air parallel to the impeller axis, providing efficient airflow in low-pressure scenarios such as cooling and exhaust systems. Both fan types leverage smart technology for enhanced energy efficiency, remote monitoring, and operational optimization, catering to diverse industrial needs and contributing to improved environmental sustainability.</p></p>
