<p><p>The high-temperature and low-sag conductor market comprises several types, each designed for enhanced performance. ACSS (Aluminum Conductor Steel Supported) combines aluminum and steel for strength and conductivity, while ACSS/TW (Aluminum Conductor Steel Supported/Twist) offers improved thermal performance. GTACSR (Galvanized Thermal Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) features galvanized steel for corrosion resistance. ACCC (Aluminum Conductor Composite Core) enhances efficiency with lightweight materials. GZTACSR (Galvanized Z-Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) combines durability with efficiency. Other types include various customized solutions for specific applications.</p></p>
