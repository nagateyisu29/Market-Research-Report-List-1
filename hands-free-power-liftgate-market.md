<p><p>The Hands-free Power Liftgate market can be divided into two main types: kicking and non-kicking systems. Kicking systems allow users to open the liftgate by simply using a kicking motion near the rear of the vehicle, providing a convenient, touch-free option, especially when hands are full. Non-kicking systems typically involve a sensor activated by a foot or a button within the vehicle, ensuring accessibility without physical contact but requiring more user input for operation. Both types enhance convenience and accessibility.</p></p>
