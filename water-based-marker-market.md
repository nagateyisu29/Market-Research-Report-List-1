<p><p>The water-based marker market comprises two primary types: Fiber Type and Foaming Type. Fiber Type markers utilize a felt tip made from synthetic or natural fibers, providing smooth and consistent ink flow for precise writing or drawing. In contrast, Foaming Type markers feature a foam tip that absorbs and dispenses ink, producing broader strokes ideal for coloring or shading. Both types are favored for their non-toxic, environmentally friendly properties, making them suitable for educational and artistic applications.</p></p>
