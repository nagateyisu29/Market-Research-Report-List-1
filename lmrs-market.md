<p><ul><li>40MHz – 174MHz (VHF)</li><li>200MHz – 512MHz (UHF)</li><li>700MHz – 1000MHz (SHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LMRS (Land Mobile Radio Services) markets operate across various frequency bands: VHF (40MHz – 174MHz), UHF (200MHz – 512MHz), and SHF (700MHz – 1000MHz). VHF is often used for regional communication with good coverage but limited bandwidth. UHF offers higher capacity and is ideal for urban environments due to its ability to penetrate buildings. SHF supports advanced communication technologies, delivering high data rates and enhanced connectivity, suitable for modern applications like public safety and commercial services.</p></p>
