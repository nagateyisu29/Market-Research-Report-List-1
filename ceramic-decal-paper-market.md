<p><strong>The Ceramic Decal Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Decals</li><li>Silkscreen Decals</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic decal paper market is segmented into three main types: digital decals, silkscreen decals, and others. Digital decals utilize inkjet or laser printing technology for high-resolution designs, making them popular for customized applications. Silkscreen decals are created by pushing ink through a mesh stencil, offering durability and vibrant colors, making them suitable for mass production. The "others" category includes techniques such as offset printing and transfer decals, catering to niche markets or specific customer needs. Each type serves unique applications in ceramics.</p></p>
