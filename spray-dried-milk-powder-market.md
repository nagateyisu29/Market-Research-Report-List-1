<p><p>The spray dried milk powder market comprises two primary types: full cream milk powder and skimmed milk powder. Full cream milk powder retains the milk's fat content, offering a rich taste and creamy texture, making it ideal for products like confections and baked goods. In contrast, skimmed milk powder has most of its fat removed, resulting in a lower calorie option suitable for health-conscious consumers and applications in beverages and dairy products. Both types cater to diverse dietary needs and preferences.</p></p>
