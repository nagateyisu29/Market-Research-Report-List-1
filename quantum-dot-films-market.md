<p><p>The Quantum Dot Films market is primarily divided into two types: Cadmium Quantum Dots and Cadmium-free Quantum Dots. Cadmium Quantum Dots contain toxic cadmium elements, offering high performance in color purity and efficiency, commonly used in displays like QLED TVs. Conversely, Cadmium-free Quantum Dots use alternative materials, providing a more environmentally friendly option while still delivering vibrant colors and improved energy efficiency. The choice between these types often hinges on regulatory considerations and sustainability preferences.</p></p>
