Outside micrometers are used for external dimensions, providing accuracy for thickness and outer diameters. Depth micrometers gauge the depth of grooves and holes, offering precise depth measurements. Special micrometers include customized or specialized devices for unique applications, catering to specific industrial or research needs. Together, these tools enhance precision in manufacturing, engineering, and quality control processes.</p></p>
