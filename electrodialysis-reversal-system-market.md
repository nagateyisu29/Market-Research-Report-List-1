<p><p>The electrodialysis reversal (EDR) system market comprises two main types: continuous electrodialysis and batch electrodialysis. Continuous electrodialysis involves a steady flow of feed solution through the system, allowing for real-time separation and recovery of ions, making it suitable for large-scale operations. In contrast, batch electrodialysis processes operate on discrete quantities of feed, ideal for smaller volumes or specific applications where flexibility is required. Both technologies are key in water desalination, wastewater treatment, and resource recovery.</p></p>
