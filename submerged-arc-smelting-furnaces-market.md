<p><ul><li>DC Submerged Arc Smelting Furnace</li><li>AC Submerged Arc Smelting Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submerged arc smelting furnace market comprises two primary types: DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current) submerged arc furnaces. DC submerged arc smelting furnaces utilize a steady direct current, providing efficient energy transfer and reduced electrode consumption, making them ideal for high-grade metallurgical operations. In contrast, AC submerged arc smelting furnaces employ alternating current, offering versatility for various applications, often used in low-to-medium grade ores. Both types are essential in the production of ferroalloys and non-ferrous metals.</p></p>
