<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) catalyst market is categorized mainly into three types: Ziegler-Natta, Metallocene, and Others. Ziegler-Natta catalysts are traditional and widely used for producing a broad range of polypropylene grades, offering good control over polymer structure. Metallocene catalysts provide precise control over molecular weight and composition, resulting in enhanced material properties. The "Others" category includes various alternative catalysts, such as single-site and chromium-based catalysts, which are utilized for niche applications and specialized polymer characteristics, broadening the market's versatility.</p></p>
