<p><p>The Pneumatic Rolling Motor market includes two primary types: Torque Controlled and Stall Torque motors. Torque Controlled motors provide adjustable torque outputs, allowing for precise control during operations, making them suitable for applications requiring variable force and speed. On the other hand, Stall Torque motors deliver maximum torque at low speeds but can stall under high load conditions. Both types are integral to industries needing efficient power transmission and control in tasks like material handling and automation processes.</p></p>
