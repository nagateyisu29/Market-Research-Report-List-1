<p><p>The nanofiber equipment market comprises several types, including melt-blown machines and electrospinning equipment. Melt-blown machines utilize high-velocity air to form fibers from molten polymer, ideal for producing filters and medical textiles. Electrospinning equipment applies high voltage to create nanofibers from polymer solutions, used in applications like tissue engineering and drug delivery. Other equipment may include needleless spinning or novel fabrication techniques, broadening the market's scope for various applications across industries such as healthcare, environmental, and textiles.</p></p>
